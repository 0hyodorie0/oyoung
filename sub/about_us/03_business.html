Remote Medical Consultation

  • O'Young application allows doctors, customers, and coordinators to have remote medical consultation wherever they are.
  • O'Young IT technology makes the customer feel as if they are at real consultation with the doctor.
  • This technology have three unique components.

Virtual outpatient
Schedule Management

The doctor opens the outpatient schedule. Customers can book a consultation schedule.

Consultation

During the consultation, the doctor can have the customer’s information synchronizing with Digital Health peripherals such as blood pressure, BMI, X-ray that allow the doctor to have a more accurate diagnosis and recommendation.

Report

Consultation reports can be captured from remote medical consultations and can be shared with the doctor and customer.

Speech Recognition Consultation Report

By O'young speech recognition and content summarization technology, consultation reports can be generated automatically for reference and further actions. Speech recognition is conducted by reading the video feed of remote consultation. Both customer and doctor do not need to summarize the consultation.

Clinic Rating based on Big Data and Algorithm

Our technology helps customers select the right clinics from true ratings, reviews, and feedback excluding advertisements.
We use the following process from IT.

Collect Data from many different sources including social networks excluding Ads.

Conduct Data analysis

Data visualization using algorithm.
