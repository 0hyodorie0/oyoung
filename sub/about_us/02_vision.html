About us

As a leading leader in beauty and health platforms based on trust and value, I want to be a healthy and beautiful partner for customers.

Vision

Forefront Leader in Health and Beauty Platform by Developing Trust and Delivering Value as a Companion in your care journey.

Trust and Value

O'young Beauty is built on the foundation of Trust. Trust is of utmost importance in O'young Beauty’s culture and is developed by delivering our own Value to customers and partners with unrivaled vision, innovation, and execution.

Health and Beauty Platform

We believe Healthcare and Beauty lead to wellness and happiness. O'young connects and engages with customers who are seeking health and beauty treatments delivered by healthcare experts who provide the highest standards of care, safety, and quality.

Forefront Leader

O'young strives to be the forefront leader to create a health and beauty ecosystem via cooperation with certified healthcare service providers in Korea and regional countries which have passed stringent screening criteria verified personally by our O'young team.

Companion

O'young is committed to be the key Companion to support your path towards wellness and happiness by ensuring customer needs are met with the best digital and physical experience across online, social and in-person interactions throughout the care journey of your health and beauty treatment.
